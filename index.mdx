draw.io Desktop is a free, offline-first diagramming application built on Electron. All diagram data stays on your machine — nothing is ever sent to external servers. It supports `.drawio` and `.vsdx` (Visio) file formats and runs on macOS, Windows, and Linux.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download draw.io Desktop">
    Download the installer for your platform from the [GitHub Releases page](https://github.com/jgraph/drawio-desktop/releases).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install and launch">
    Run the installer and open draw.io Desktop. A blank diagram canvas opens automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first diagram">
    Drag shapes from the sidebar onto the canvas, connect them with arrows, and add labels. Use **File > Save** to save your diagram as a `.drawio` file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export your work">
    Go to **File > Export As** to export your diagram as PDF, PNG, SVG, or HTML.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  draw.io Desktop is completely free under the Apache 2.0 license. No account required, no telemetry, no data leaves your machine.
</Note>
